CodeRoom is a code sharing website designed to keep your code formatted correctly as you share it.
Simply login using your Google account, create a room, input your code snippet, and post your code.
Your unique Google ID is the key to your room, all you have to do is share it with whoever you want to give access to your code.
After entering it, your fellow developers can access the formatted code until you post a new snippet.
